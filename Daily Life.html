Daily Life in Haarlem

Get familiar with the rhythm of life in Haarlem. Here you’ll find information about public transport, grocery stores, and daily necessities.

Public Transport

Haarlem is well connected with buses, trains, and bikes. Get an OV-chipkaart to travel conveniently around the city and beyond.

Shops & Markets

Find supermarkets like Albert Heijn, Jumbo, and Lidl across the city. Don’t miss the Saturday market at Grote Markt for fresh produce.
