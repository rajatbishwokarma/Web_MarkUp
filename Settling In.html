Settling In

Once you've arrived, there are a few important steps to complete so you can start your new life smoothly.

Registration

Register at the Haarlem municipality (gemeente) to receive your BSN number — necessary for health insurance and employment.

Healthcare

Register with a local GP (huisarts). In the Netherlands, your GP is the first contact for medical care and referrals.
